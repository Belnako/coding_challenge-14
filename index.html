Rules

Join two numbers of the same amount together to add them horizontally or vertically by Swiping Up, Down, Left or Right (for mobile) or using the arrow Keys on your keyboard for PC.

Goal of the game is to reach the number 2048.

You lose when every square on the borad is filled with an number before reaching 2048.

Your game is saved automatically. You can close your browser and return to yout game later, by clicking the button: "Load saved game".
