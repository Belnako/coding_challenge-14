Rules

Turn two cards, if they show the same flower they will stay turned, if not they will flip back.

You can only flip two cards at once and have to wait until they flip back to try another one.

Your moves to solve this memory will be counted and stored, so you can challenge yourself into beating your highscore with less moves!
